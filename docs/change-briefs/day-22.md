# Day 22 – Universal Document Import v1

## What Changed

PathOS now has an **Import Center** where you can bring in documents and files from your computer – not just emails.

### New Import Options

1. **Email Import Inbox** (same as before)
   - Paste email content or upload .eml files
   - Automatically categorizes job notices, pay stubs, and more

2. **Files & Text Import** (new!)
   - **Upload files**: Drag and drop PDF, Word documents (.docx), or text files
   - **Paste text**: Copy and paste any text (like a job posting from a website)
   - **Automatic categories**: PathOS guesses what type of document it is
   - **Privacy labels**: Mark documents as Public, Personal, or Sensitive

### What You Can Do With Imported Documents

- **View** – Open and read PDFs and text files right in PathOS
- **Rename** – Give documents better names
- **Tag** – Add your own labels
- **Change category** – Fix the automatic guess if it's wrong
- **Reset** – Clear all tags and labels without deleting the file
- **Delete** – Remove documents you don't need

### Privacy Features

- Each document has its own visibility toggle (hide/show)
- Global privacy mode still works across all documents
- "Delete All Local Data" now clears all imported documents too

---

## Why It Matters

Before Day 22, you could only import email files (.eml format). Most people don't save emails that way – they have PDFs of job postings, Word documents with offer letters, or text they copied from websites.

Now you can:
- Save a job posting PDF from USAJOBS and import it
- Copy text from any website and save it in PathOS
- Keep all your job search documents in one place

---

## How to Use It

1. Click **"Import Center"** in the sidebar (under IMPORT)
2. Choose a tab:
   - **Email Import** – for email content
   - **Files & Text** – for PDFs, documents, and pasted text
3. Drag files into the upload area, or click to browse
4. Optionally paste text into the text area
5. Click "Import" and your document appears in the list

---

## Important Notes

- **OPSEC reminder**: Don't import classified or CUI content
- **Local storage only**: Files stay on your computer – they're not uploaded anywhere
- **Word documents**: For now, you can only download them (preview coming later)
