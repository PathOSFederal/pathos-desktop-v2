# Day 42 — Benefits Comparison Workspace v1

## What Changed
- Added a dedicated Benefits Comparison Workspace at `/explore/benefits/workspace`.
- Introduced an immersive workspace-style layout for benefits exploration.
- Added “Ask PathAdvisor” actions on key workspace cards to request explanations.
- Added a clear Exit control so users can return to the Benefits overview page.

## Why It Matters
- Gives users a focused place to explore benefits scenarios without getting lost in the dashboard.
- Establishes the foundation for future benefits intelligence (cost estimates, coverage highlights, and deeper comparisons).
- Improves usability by making it obvious how to leave the workspace and return to the overview.

## What to Notice
- From the Benefits overview, users can open the new workspace and see a larger comparison experience.
- Workspace includes an Exit action back to the overview.
- Clicking “Ask PathAdvisor” on workspace cards triggers PathAdvisor behavior (explanations are stubbed/early-stage).
